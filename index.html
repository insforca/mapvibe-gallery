Our Process

Real maps,
refined.

Every MapVibe print begins with raw OpenStreetMap cartographic data for your city. We strip it down to its essential geometry — streets, districts, coastlines — then apply precision-tuned palettes for quiet, interior-quality results.

Why MapVibe

  • Generated from real geographic data — your city, not a stock illustration
  • Print-resolution files (300 dpi) ready for any size up to 90×120 cm
  • Palettes designed for Scandinavian, Japandi, and editorial interior styles
  • Instant digital delivery — frame it anywhere in the world
